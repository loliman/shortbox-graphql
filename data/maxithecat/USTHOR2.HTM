Thor (II)  The Mighty Thor (II) (1998-2004) (PSR)

Series is named "Thor" in the imprint, but most covers have the title "The Mighty Thor".
Covers feature title "The Mighty Thor Lord of Asgard" from #45 to #67 (with a few exceptions), then "The Mighty Thor Lord of Earth" until #79 (again with a few exceptions).
Rating of series is "PG" up to #67, then changes to "PSR" with #68.

Marvels: Eye of the Camera (2009-2010) (A)

In Wizard Magazine #0 (Sep 2003) there is a 2-page article about a planned sequel to Marvels titled "Marvels: Eye to the Camera". It was announced to be six issues, written by Kurt Busiek, painted by Jay Anacleto and digitally colored by Brian Haberlin, and was planned to be published at the end of 2003 or early 2004. The issues would have been focusing on:
•#1 - The Trial of Yellowjacket (Avengers (I) #228-230)
•#2 - The Dark Phoenix Saga (Uncanny X-Men (I) #234-237)
•#3 - The Punisher's Debut (The Amazing Spider-Man (I) #129)
•#4 - The Death of Elektra (Daredevil (I) #181)
•#5 - The Death of the X-Men in Dallas (Uncanny X-Men (I) #225-227)
•#6 - Secret Wars II (Secret Wars II #1-9).
In 2009 this project finally was published.

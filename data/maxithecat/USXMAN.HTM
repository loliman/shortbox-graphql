X MAN 
X-Man (1995-2001)

Series started during the months of "Age of Apocalypse" (#1 to #4, replacing Cable (I)), but turned over to the main Marvel Universe with #5 as an additional series.
Series was published with the "Counter-X" brand from #63 on.
